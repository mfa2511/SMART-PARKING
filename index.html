AI Architecture & Mathematical Models

Multiple Linear Regression

Our supervised learning model evaluates feature weights (Hour, Day, Rain, Exam) to predict exact available spots. The trained equation clearly highlights the massive negative impact (-6.4) of exam days.

Y(Available) = 42.7
  - 2.1 * (Hour)
  - 1.8 * (Day_Num)
  - 6.4 * (Is_Exam_Day)
  - 1.2 * (Is_Rainy_Day)

ARIMA(1,1,1) Time Series

To capture the cyclic daily demand of campus arrivals and departures, we utilize the AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average framework to forecast the next 5 hourly slots.

  • AR (1): Analyzes dependency on past hourly availability.
  • I (1): First-order differencing makes the parking trend stationary.
  • MA (1): Smooths out sudden unpredictable surges (e.g., event endings).
